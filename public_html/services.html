Overview.

Meathway Contacting Inc is a well established General Contractor servicing the New York City Metro Area; all boroughs, including Long Island.
Since 2005 we have been delivering projects on time and on budget with the highest standards of quality and service.

We can renovate any area of your home or business, and coordinate all aspects of your remodeling or new construction project.

We can restore any property and have experience with town houses, penthouses, apartments, lofts, and commercial spaces. We will provide you with accurate and firm estimates. We will take care of everything from your plumbing and electrical needs, add or remove walls, refinish or replace flooring, and install built-in furniture, cabinetry, appliances, tiles, and countertops.

Our workers are highly skilled, courteous, and meticulous. Your satisfaction is our highest goal: we hope that you will recommend us to your neighbors, friends, and relatives.
